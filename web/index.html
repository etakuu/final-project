Costa Rica

Costa Rica is located in North America. It borders Panama and Nicuragua.San Jose is Costa Ricas capital.

Wild Life

For being such a small country its bursting with wildlife. Around the same size as West Virginia it still hosts 3 different types of rainforests. Costa Rica is home to over 500,000 different species. About 4 percent of all plant, insect and wildlife species on Earth.

History

Costa Rica started off as a largely agricultural country it is now relying on industry, services, and eco-tourism. Many belive that soon tourism will soon be their major contributor to the nations GDP..

Historic Places

Arenal Volcano

The most active volcano in Costa Rica.

Green Iguana

The Green iguana, also known as the American iguana is a mostly herbivrous species of lizard of the genus Iguana.

Famous Hanging Bridges

Arenal Hanging Bridges are suspension bridges in the Volcano.

Costa Rican Coffee

In Costa Rica it is illegal to produce any type of coffee other than 100% Arabica coffee beans.In 1989 Costa Rica passseed a law that prohibited the planting of low quality beans, encouraging Costa Rican farmers to purse excellence.

animals

Pura Vida

Translated into "pure life", this phrase has much meaning to Costa Rican culture. Ticos/Ticas use it to say "take it easy"," enjo life", "all good", "hello", "goodbye". Many foreigners truly dont haave a true grasp of the meaning as they are not Costa Ricans themselves.

People

President

Luis Guillermo Solís

A Costa Rican republican who has been president of Costa Rica since 2014. Luis is a member of the center-left Citizens' Action party.

Astronaut

Franklin Chang Díaz

Franklin Chang Díaz is a Costa Rican-American mechanical engineer, physicist and former NASA astronaut.He is a veteran of seven Space Shuttle missions. He is of Chinese and Costa Rican Spanish descent.
